Get Started

We know you're excited to get started with the really cool stuff like flipkicks and hitting the ramps, but in Skateboarding, the foundations are crutial! If you can nail these foundational moves, you're ready for the next tricks!

Starter Skills

Now that you've mastered the foundational skills, you're ready to move on! These tricks will help you furthur your skills, easily get around, and be prepared for the more difficult tricks.

Ollie

This skill is a necessity for jumping onto curbs, over objects in the road, and as a basis for advanced skills.
